About

Sheek Bakrii Saphaloo Translator

Purpose

Sheek Bakrii Saphaloo (ሽክ ባክሪ ሳፋሉ) is a transliteration tool designed to help speakers of Afar and related Oromo dialects convert text between Latin Qubee (Latin script) and Sheek Bakrii Saphaloo (Ethiopian script).

This tool is built for researchers, students, language enthusiasts, and anyone interested in preserving and sharing the Sheek Bakrii script in the digital age.

Credits & Inspiration

This project was inspired by the research and documentation of Oreen Yousuf & Daniel Yacob and their proposal for encoding the Sheek Bakrii Saphaloo script in the Unicode Standard.

We acknowledge and thank the researchers who developed and codified this script. This tool is a simple implementation to help make the script more accessible.
